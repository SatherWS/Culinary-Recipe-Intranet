Welcome to Killer Recipes.

We provide the raw information you need to make great meals.

Why does this app exist?

I wanted to provide a simple collection of easy to read food recipes. Too many cooking websites are littered with ads and loosely related personal anecdotes. Our site simply provides the steps and ingredients needed for each dish. Our collection is gathered from various sources from our web scraper.

Free & Open Source

Killer Recipes is purely an informational website, we do not require user accounts nor use any location trackers. Check us out on GitHub!

Education

We're working on building a robust library of recipes. Our goal is to deliver easy to follow recipes for all to enjoy.

Submissions

Your recipes are welcomed here, feel free to email them to info@griller.recipes or reach out through our social media accounts.
