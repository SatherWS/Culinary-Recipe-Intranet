An awesome collection of recipes!


Why does this exist?

I wanted to provide a simple collection of easy to read food recipes. Too many cooking websites are littered with ads and loosely related personal anecdotes. Our site simply provides the steps and ingredients needed for each dish. Our collection is gathered from our web scraper and user submitted recipes.

Free & Open Source

Killer Recipes is purely an informational website, we do not require user accounts nor use any location trackers.

Education

We're working on building a robust library of recipes. Our goal is to deliver easy to follow recipes for all to enjoy.

Submissions

Your recipes are welcomed here, feel free to email them to info@griller.recipes or reach out through our social media accounts.
