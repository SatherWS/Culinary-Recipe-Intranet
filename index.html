Why does this website exist?

I used to be a line cook at a deli. It sucked but, I'd have the privilege of cooking great meals for myself, friends, and family. Now as a programmer I find myself exclusively eating prepared foods and eating out way too much. I miss the activity of preparing a meals for groups of people to enjoy together. Perhaps if I build a large database of awesome recipes I'll regain the motivation to get back into cooking.

Free & Open Source

Killer Recipes is purely an informational website, we do not require user accounts nor use any location trackers.

Education

We're working on building a robust library of recipes. Our goal is to deliver easy to follow recipes for all to enjoy.
